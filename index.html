MENTALLMRN

Mental Health Large Language Model Research Network

The MENTALLMRN is a scientific network of researchers united by their shared interest in large language models and their potential for advancing mental health research. Through interdisciplinary collaboration, we develop new methods, exchange scientific findings, and jointly deepen the understanding of these systems. Our aim is to open up innovative perspectives and sustainably advance research in this rapidly evolving field.
